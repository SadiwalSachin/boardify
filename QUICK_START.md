## üìñ Quick Tutorial (30 seconds)

### Step 1: Open Your Board
Navigate to: http://localhost:5173

### Step 2: Try Panning
```
1. Hold down the SPACEBAR key
2. You'll see: "Pan Mode Active - Click and drag to move canvas"

4. Release SPACEBAR to draw again
```

### Step 3: Try Zooming
```
1. Scroll your mouse wheel UP to zoom in
2. Scroll your mouse wheel DOWN to zoom out
3. Watch the percentage in the bottom-right corner
```

### Step 4: Draw Everywhere!
```
1. Select the Pencil tool
2. Draw something
3. Hold SPACEBAR and pan to a new area
4. Draw more content
5. Zoom out to see everything
```

## üí° Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Use Spacebar for Quick Navigation
Instead of scrolling, hold spacebar and drag - it's faster!

### Tip 2: Zoom to Your Cursor
When you zoom with mouse wheel, it zooms toward your cursor position. Point at what you want to see!

### Tip 3: Work in Sections
For large projects:
- Create sections in different areas
- Use pan to jump between them
- Zoom out occasionally to see the big picture

### Tip 4: Don't Worry About Getting Lost
If you can't find your drawing:
1. Click the Reset Zoom button (maximize icon)
2. Or zoom way out to see everything

### Tip 5: Combine Pan and Zoom
- Zoom in for details
- Pan to navigate
- Zoom out for overview
- Repeat!

## üêõ Quick Troubleshooting

### Problem: Can't pan
**Solution:** Make sure you're holding SPACEBAR, not just pressing it

### Problem: Canvas won't move
**Solution:** Check if you're in text editing mode - press ESC first

### Problem: Lost my drawing
**Solution:** Click the Reset Zoom button (maximize icon) or zoom way out

### Problem: Zoom too sensitive
**Solution:** Use the +/- buttons instead of mouse wheel
